About Us

Since 2008, we've been commited to serving the healthiest meals in Riverside County.

No Fryer, No Freezers, No Microwave, No heat-lamps. Make almost anything on the menu dairy Free, vegan and/or gluten free.

Ensuring the utmost quality and freshness, just about everything we serve is made from scratch at our Earth Bistro kitchen.

We source the best ingredients, always free of preservatives, food colorings, flavor enhancers and GMOs. Our produce is locally sourced when possible, and all meat is humanely raised without antibiotics or hormones.
